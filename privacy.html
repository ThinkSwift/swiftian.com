Privacy Policy

Swiftian respects your privacy. Our apps are designed to help you practice and enjoy language learning without collecting unnecessary personal data.

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you choose to contact us directly. Any data processed within the app stays on your device, unless explicitly stated otherwise.

We may use standard analytics tools to understand general usage patterns and improve the app experience.
